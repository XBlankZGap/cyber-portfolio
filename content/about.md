### Professional Profile
I am a cybersecurity specialist dedicated to infrastructure protection, offensive security analysis, and secure system design. With a background in defensive laboratory configuration and proactive threat hunting, I build secure digital environments while identifying critical vulnerabilities before they can be exploited.

### Core Expertise

- **Defensive Infrastructure:** Secure network design, virtualization, and administrative hardening.
- **Data Protection:** Database security, SQL interrogation, and offline cryptanalysis.

### My Approach
I believe in a security-first methodology that combines deep technical implementation with strategic risk management. Every project in this portfolio represents a hands-on exploration of complex vulnerabilities and the corresponding defensive solutions.
